WHO AM I?

I'm a student
Pursuing a combined major in Business and Computer Science (BUCS), this distinctive program offered by the Sauder School of Business teaches both the business acumen and technical skills that allows me to be immersed and thrive in business and tech environments. Having just finished my second year, I am excited to be starting an 8 month co-op term in September!

I'm a musician
Since I picked up my first pair of drumsticks 7 years ago, I haven't been able to put them down and, consequently, music has been a passion of mine ever since. I've had the privilege of performing at small local cafes, to the largest Jazz Festival on the West Coast of Canada and I'm able to continue this passion as a member of the UBC Jazz Ensemble.

I'm a discoverer
I've worked on the ground floor, looking through an oven full of pizzas to working on the 23rd floor, looking out to the neighbouring towers of downtown Vancouver. I'm well used to the claustrophobia that can come with playing squash, but no matter how many times I go rock climbing, I can't resist the exhilarating feeling of hanging upside down.

Above all else,
I am constantly seeking new experiences to meet new people and find new heights to which I can grow.
